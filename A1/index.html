Pokemon Old Gen I

Mega Charizard X

Mega Charizard X is a dual-type Fire/Dragon Pokémon activated by the Charizardite X stone, transforming Charizard into a black-and-light-blue beast with blue flames, increased Attack/Defense, and the Tough Claws ability. It is heavily focused on physical offense, differing from the special-attacking Mega Charizard Y, making it a formidable Dragon Dancer in competitive play.
